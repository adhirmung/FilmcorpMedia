Frequently Asked Questions

Real answers to the questions small business owners actually ask

How much does this actually cost?
We know "it depends" is annoying, so here's some real talk: our photography packages typically start from R2,500 for small shoots, product photography from R150-300 per product depending on volume, traditional video projects from around R5,000 for social content, and AI-generated videos from R1,500. We'll give you a clear quote before any work starts — no surprises.
How long until I get my content?
For photography: typically within 5-7 working days after your shoot. Traditional video takes a bit longer — usually 7-14 working days depending on complexity. AI video generation is much faster — often 24-48 hours from brief to final video. Need something faster? Let us know and we'll see what's possible.
What's AI video generation, and how does it work?
AI video generation uses cutting-edge artificial intelligence to create professional videos from scripts, images, or concepts — no traditional filming required. Perfect for explainer videos, product demos, ads, and social content. It's faster and more affordable than traditional video shoots, with turnaround times as quick as 24-48 hours. Think of it as your budget-friendly option for getting professional video content when you need it fast or don't need a physical shoot.
I've never done a professional shoot before. Is that okay?
Absolutely — many of our clients are first-timers. We'll guide you through the whole process, from what to wear to how to prepare your products. Part of our job is making you comfortable, so don't stress about not knowing what to do.
Will you come to my location, or do I need to come to you?
We do both. We can shoot at your business premises, at our studio, or at a location that makes sense for your brand. Wherever tells your story best — we'll make it work.
My budget is really limited right now. Can you still help?
We'd rather do something within your budget than not work together at all. Tell us what you can spend, and we'll tell you honestly what's possible. Sometimes we can get creative with smaller scopes, and sometimes we'll recommend you wait until the budget stretches further. No pressure either way.
Do I own the content you create?
Yes. Once the project is paid for, you own full rights to use the content however you need — website, social media, ads, printed materials, the works. No hidden licensing fees or usage restrictions.
